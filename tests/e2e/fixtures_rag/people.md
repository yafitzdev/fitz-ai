# People and Organizations

This document contains information about key people and organizations for testing entity graph and multi-hop retrieval.

## Sarah Chen

Sarah Chen is the CEO of TechCorp Industries. She joined the company in 2019 after spending 8 years at AutoMotors where she served as VP of Engineering. Sarah holds a PhD in Electrical Engineering from MIT and is known for her expertise in battery technology.

## TechCorp Industries

TechCorp Industries is a leading manufacturer of electric vehicles and advanced battery systems. The company was founded in 2015 and is headquartered in Austin, Texas. TechCorp's product lineup includes the Model X100, Model Y200, and Model Z50 series of electric vehicles.

Key facts about TechCorp:
- Founded: 2015
- Headquarters: Austin, Texas
- Employees: 5,200
- Products: Electric vehicles and batteries
- Main competitor: GreenDrive Inc.

## GreenDrive Inc

GreenDrive Inc is a major competitor to TechCorp Industries. The company specializes in hydrogen fuel cell vehicles rather than battery-electric vehicles. GreenDrive was founded in 2012 and is headquartered in San Jose, California.

Key facts about GreenDrive:
- Founded: 2012
- Headquarters: San Jose, California
- CEO: Marcus Webb
- Annual revenue: $2.3 billion
- Products: Hydrogen fuel cell vehicles

## Marcus Webb

Marcus Webb is the CEO of GreenDrive Inc. He previously worked at Toyota's fuel cell division before founding GreenDrive. Marcus is a strong advocate for hydrogen technology as an alternative to battery-electric vehicles.

## AutoMotors

AutoMotors is a traditional automotive manufacturer that produces gasoline-powered vehicles. Sarah Chen worked there as VP of Engineering before joining TechCorp Industries. The company has been slow to transition to electric vehicles.
