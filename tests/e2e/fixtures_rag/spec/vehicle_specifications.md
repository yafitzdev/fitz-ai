## Official Warranty Terms

All TechCorp vehicles come with the following official warranty coverage:

1. **Battery and Drivetrain**: 8 years / 150,000 miles
2. **Comprehensive**: 4 years / 50,000 miles
3. **Corrosion**: 12 years / unlimited miles
4. **Roadside Assistance**: 4 years / unlimited miles

## Official Service Requirements

- First Service: 12,500 miles or 12 months
- Subsequent Services: Every 12,500 miles or 12 months
- Brake Fluid: Replace every 2 years
- Cabin Air Filter: Replace every 12 months
