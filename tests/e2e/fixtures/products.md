# Product Catalog

This document contains detailed specifications for TechCorp's electric vehicle lineup.

## Model X100

The Model X100 is TechCorp's mid-range electric sedan designed for everyday commuting.

### Specifications
- Price: $45,000
- Range: 300 miles per charge
- Battery capacity: 75 kWh
- 0-60 mph: 5.2 seconds
- Top speed: 140 mph

### Features
- Standard autopilot
- Fast charging (10-80% in 25 minutes)
- 15-inch touchscreen display
- Leather seats
- Premium sound system

### Colors Available
- Pearl White
- Midnight Black
- Ocean Blue

## Model Y200

The Model Y200 is TechCorp's premium electric SUV with extended range and luxury features.

### Specifications
- Price: $55,000
- Range: 400 miles per charge
- Battery capacity: 100 kWh
- 0-60 mph: 4.5 seconds
- Top speed: 155 mph

### Features
- Advanced autopilot with full self-driving capability
- Premium audio system by Bang & Olufsen
- Solar roof option
- Heated and ventilated seats
- 17-inch touchscreen display
- Heads-up display

### Colors Available
- Pearl White
- Midnight Black
- Forest Green
- Sunset Red

## Model Z50

The Model Z50 is TechCorp's entry-level electric vehicle designed for budget-conscious consumers.

### Specifications
- Price: $35,000
- Range: 200 miles per charge
- Battery capacity: 50 kWh
- 0-60 mph: 6.8 seconds
- Top speed: 120 mph

### Features
- Basic autopilot (lane keeping, adaptive cruise)
- 10-inch touchscreen display
- Cloth seats
- Standard sound system

### Colors Available
- Pearl White
- Midnight Black

## Battery Technology

All TechCorp vehicles use proprietary lithium-ion battery cells manufactured at our Gigafactory in Nevada. The batteries feature:
- Advanced thermal management
- 8-year / 150,000 mile warranty
- Fast charging capability
- Industry-leading energy density
